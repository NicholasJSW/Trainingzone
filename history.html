The founder of the company, Terrence Zonas is still CEO and spokesperson for Training Zone. Here is one of his first ever training lessons, the world-famous Lecture Clash training:
Anyone got a lecture clash? They're not pretty, but with your help, they can be manageable. Here are some thoughts on how to deal with it:

    You are the one who is responsible for getting work done.
  • Attending a lecture and gathering notes from others
  • Find a friend in a lecture that you missed and get them to tell you what else was covered taught in that lecture.
  • Which one/s did you miss? - A few ideas to help you with making this decision are:
    • If you miss one out of two lectures for a subject, that's 50%. If you miss one out of 3 that's 33%.
    • Which one are you most ahead of?
    • Which subject has the most complete lecture notes?
  • Don't ask your lecturer - "Is there anything important that I missed?" If you didn't miss anything, there'd be no point to lectures.
